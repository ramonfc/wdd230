White Space

Google google.com

Google's home page doesn't have elements filling every space. It's remarkable the concentration is in the middle, and how includes free space.

PARC: Contrast

GitHub github.com

The colors that GitHub's site uses let us realize the difference between foreground and background. Also helps to easily read the content. The elements seem to work together to form an entity.

PARC: Alignment

Amazon amazon.com

Visiting Amazon's site the alignment of the elements shows an easy way to look for content. The structure is well-positioned. Content appears in spaces where it is expected.
