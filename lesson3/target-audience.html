Target Audience

In Manizales, there are many talented people, who want to be recognized and help others through their products and services. The target of this site is the local entrepreneurs to help get the means to show their growing enterprises.

Persona 1

Katherin

She is a young mother that with her husband started a little baking. The idea started when she wanted to prepare a cake for the birthday of her kid. Now the enterprise involves her parents and more relatives.

Persona 2

Daniel

He started an idea to prepare fruits marmalade. He and his wife decided to start with this because they wanted a healthy way to enjoy this product, they noticed that in the market many of these products are made with a lot of chemicals.
