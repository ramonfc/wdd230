Cathedral

The current Manizales Cathedral is the result of the international competition held for the presentation of plans after three fires that shook the city in 1922, 1925 and 1926, construction began on February 5, 1928. Construction took a total of 11 years. Maximum height 115 meters.

The Monument to the Settlers

The Monument to the Settlers located in the Cyprus neighborhood, on October 12 Avenue, began to be built between 1997 and 2002, in the area where the famous Aranguito Lake existed years ago.

Nevado del Ruíz

Since the 16th century there are specific records of the volcanic activity of the Nevado del Ruiz, although it has an eruptive history of 1.8 million years. The Nevado, 5,321 meters high and located on the border of the departments of Caldas and Tolima, has a diameter at its base of 15 kilometers in length.
